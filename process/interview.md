# Interview?

## 16 July 2026

I managed to get hold of Maksim Bugrimov via email (it worked when I sent via my Concordia address). He was very kind in terms of being supportive of me making the project itself, and even said he was open to answering some questions, so I'd like to line up some questions and fire them off. My thought it to ask him something about each work and I focused on that while writing the [Curation](./curation.md) document, so let me list them here and maybe even send them and see what happens...

### Questions v.1 (and only?)

*PBR Pizza*  
https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/3d/props/food/pbr-pizza-108425

This is the only item of food in your catalog, a pepperoni pizza using physically based rendering. Is this a real pizza you eventually ate, or did it come from somewhere else? Why *this* pizza, and why a pizza at all?

*Desert Night*  
https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/3d/environments/landscapes/desert-night-77444

This is the only terrain in your catalog, and it's very beautiful. Why did you decide to set this desert at night specifically? Is it a real place, and did you see it by night?

*Humanoid Creatures Pack*  
https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/3d/characters/creatures/humanoid-creatures-pack-162689

Of all your work, this is the thing I'm most connected to because on the asset store page the playthrough of their animations makes it look to me like they're dancing. Monsters of different kinds are a huge part of your work, what draws you to modelling them specifically?

*Base Man*  
https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/3d/characters/base-man-133457

When you make a human figure like this, are you thinking about them as a real person? Who is this guy? Where is he from?

*Stalin Statue*  
https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/3d/props/exterior/stalin-statue-78188

I'm interested in this one because it obviously connects both to the idea of you as a *sculptor* specifically, and to the history of Russia. What led you to model Stalin in this way, how do you imagine it showing up in a game?

*Marine cargo ship*  
https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/3d/environments/industrial/marine-cargo-ship-63770

This model is as huge as you would expect, but it also weighs nothing and has nothing inside it. How do you think about the emptiness inside most 3D models, especially when they're this massive?

*Pictures*  
https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/3d/props/interior/pictures-88112

These look to me like two pictures of the Annunciation, where are the images themselves from and does that scene have a specific significance for you?

*Pipers* and *Barrels*  
https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/3d/environments/industrial/pipers-63800  
https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/3d/environments/industrial/barrels-63623

Some of the work you have made is clearly designed to be in the background and part of a larger scene, not necessarily to be noticed on its own. Have you ever seen work like this of your in a game – how did that feel, or how do you think it would feel?

*Dead Man in the Bag*  
https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/3d/environments/industrial/dead-man-in-the-bag-79484

This is my other absolute favourite work of yours because it reminds me of the work of artist Gregor Schneider who makes uncanny, horror-like art installations. What draws you to this kind of horror imagery?

*Spaceship*  
https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/3d/props/industrial/spaceship-33345

This very polished model is actually quite unusual in your work because it's in a cartoon style you don't often use. How do you choose the specific aesthetic approach you're going to adopt – why did you decide to make a handful of cartoon-style objects like this?

*Apocalypse Pack*  
https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/3d/environments/industrial/apocalypse-pack-58281

This is an incredibly wealth of objects (including the cargo ship!). I'm curious both about your relationship to the idea of an apocalypse (imagined or in our future) as well as how you imagine somebody really using a pack like this to put together their own apocalyptic setting in a game.
